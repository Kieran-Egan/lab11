What does it mean to sit and stare? Does one really ever fall asleep? These are a few of the many questions answered in this month's edition of Kieran's Wacky Ad Libs. Some may say these are silly and pointless questions. And yet, my answer to these criticisms is to present a new question altogether: do questions hold inherent meaning? I would argue that some questions can simply exist as a fun thought experiment. There isn't a single correct answer, but an attempt to provoke contemplation. If that is achieved in my Ad Libs, then I will be nothing but ecstatic.
